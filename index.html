About Me

Adrian Ash is a professional musician, bandleader, arranger, and educator specializing in 4, 5, and 6-string bass guitar and upright bass, as well as drums, symphonic and ethnic percussion, ukulele, banjo, mandolin, piano, and voice. He is also a capable audio engineer, both live and in the studio. He is the father of two amazing children, Eli (13) and Rose (8). He received his Bachelor of Science in Music Technology and Management from Georgia State University in 2007, and his Master of Business Administration in International Business from Georgia State University in 2011. Professional interests include data management and analysis, web development, and business management systems.

When he's not hosting a tribute jam with his vast network of Atlanta area musicians known as BadAsh Allstar Team, or performing with one of his many different bands, he can usually be found doing yard work or melting aluminum, brass, and copper recyclables into art in his back yard.
